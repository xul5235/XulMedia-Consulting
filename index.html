Let's Talk

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

A standard Photography Session is a half-day shoot — roughly 3 to 4 hours at your location. We handle setup, styling, and breakdown within that window. For larger projects or retainer clients, we plan the schedule together.

You receive 15–20 fully retouched, high-resolution images ready to use. Raw files are not included by default — the editing is part of the value. If you need raws for a specific reason, we can discuss it.

Delivery within 5 business days from the day of the shoot. Retainer clients get priority editing and typically receive content within 3 business days.

Primarily Oslo-based, but open to projects in the greater Oslo region. For locations further away, travel costs may apply. Get in touch and we'll work something out.

We're onboarding our first 3 long-term clients at a reduced monthly rate. In exchange, we ask for an honest testimonial and the right to feature the work as a case study. The work quality is the same — the price reflects the early stage of the partnership.
